Added multiple fee systems
  * Opening  - When you open an account           (player pays the fee)
  * Deposit  - When you deposit to an account     (player pays the fee)
  * Withdraw - When you withdraw from an account  (account pays the fee)
  * Transfer - When you transfer between accounts (sender account pays the fee)
  * Closing  - When you close an account          (account pays the fee)
  * Sign     - When you create a balance sign     (player pays the fee)
